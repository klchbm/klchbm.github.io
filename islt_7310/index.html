Welcome to my portfolio website!


The purpose of this site is to get a sense of who I am beyond my paper resume. Just four short years ago I was envisioning where I could fit in the tech industry. I was introduced to computer science in my senior year of high school. I chose to study IT in college and wondered what I could do with it in the future. Would I be developing applications? Helping solve real-world problems? Inspiring others like me? Luckily, I've had the opportunity to do all three. Thanks to the IT program at the University of Missouri, I have been shown endless opportunities to advance my skills. From attending conferences across the country, participating in STEM outreach, to working at a global tech company! I have been equipped with the tools needed to begin my journey. I welcome you to explore my website. Feel free to reach out and ask questions through my contacts page.
